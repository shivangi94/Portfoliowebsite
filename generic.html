Hello & Welcome!

My name is Shivangi Cial.
I am an International student at UT Dallas, currently pursuing Master of science in Management Information Systems with a focus on Business Intelligence and Data Analytics track.
Back in India, I did my Bachelors in Technology in Computer Engineering from Aligarh Muslim University. I joined Tata Consultancy Services after graduation and worked for about 4 years as a System Engineer - Business Intelligence and Data Integration Analyst. I worked on tools like Oracle Data Integrator, Microsoft SQL Server, Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, JIRA and ServiceNow. I love to play with Data. I enjoy working on projects which involve Data Visualization using Python, SQL and Tableau. I designed this website to show case my portfolio projects. Apart from being a Data enthusiast, I enjoy communicating with people, exploring the new country I am in "United States of America". Please feel free to contact me if you have similar interest or enjoy baking/cooking in general!
